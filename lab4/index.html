Pseudocoding an everyday task outline

  • Making a pizza (assuming you don't make from scratch)
  • Get your dough, sauce, cheese and whatever toppings you like
  • Start to preheat oven
  • Shape the dough, then add sauce and cheese, then add toppings

  • When done, take out pizza and let it cool for a few minutes before cutting with pizza cutter
